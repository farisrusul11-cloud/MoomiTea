Our story

Family-run, freshly poured.

Moomi Tea is a family-owned bubble tea shop tucked inside Underwood Marketplace. Every cup is made to order — fresh milk, real fruit, and hand-cooked pearls — with the sugar and ice always dialled to how you like it.

Whether you're after a classic pearl milk tea or something a little more adventurous like our cheese foam range, our team's happy to help you find your new favourite.
